# IASPEI Summer School 2025

This repository contains all the lectures and training materials for the [IASPEI Early Career Scientists School 2025](https://iaga-iaspei-2025.org/early-career-school/), from 25 to 29 of August.

This training school is intendeed for graduate students, postdoctoral researchers, and professionals in the fields of seismology, geophysics, and related disciplines who wish to enhance their expertise in Ocean Bottom Seismometers.
